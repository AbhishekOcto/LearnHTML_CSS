MOTIHARI COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

Govt. of Bihar (Dept. of Science and Technology)

Motihari College of Engineering (MCE), Motihari is a government college, fully funded by the government of Bihar. It is affiliated to Aryabhatta Knowledge University, Patna and approved by AICTE (New Delhi). Previously the institute was known as Indian College of Engineering and was established on 24 November 1980. In 1986, the college was taken over by the Bihar government. Later in November 2008, the Government reopen the institute & the name was changed from Indian College of Engineering to Motihari College of Engineering.

  • B.Tech Programs available in:
  • 1.Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • 2.Computer Science Engineering
  • 3.Mechanical Engineering
  • 4.Civil Engineering

Mission of the Institution

  • 1. To inspire socially and economically backward students upon a strong foundation of secular, humanitarian world view.
  • 2. To make the teaching learning process simpler and stronger.
  • 3. To provide quality education to our students.
  • 4. To produce graduates to peruse engineering professions and seek collaboration with institutions of higher learning of repute to upgrade and update the quality of the institution.
  • 5. To create good future citizens for the nation of capability, commitment, conscience and compassion.
